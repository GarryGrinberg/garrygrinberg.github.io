Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Life Path number?
Your Life Path number is the most important number in numerology. It's calculated from your full date of birth and represents your core personality, your natural strengths, and the challenges you're here to overcome. Think of it as your soul's operating system.
What are the Master Numbers 11, 22, and 33?
In the GG33 system, 11, 22, and 33 are Master Numbers — they are not reduced further because they carry amplified energy. Life Path 11 is the Master Intuitive, 22 is the Master Builder, and 33 is the Master Influencer. These are the rarest and most powerful paths.
How is the Lucky Number calculated?
The GG33 Lucky Number is derived from a specific formula using your birth month's first digit and your birth year's last non-zero digit. This creates a personalized two-digit lucky code that operates as your energetic signature for the year.
Is this the same system Garry Grinberg teaches?
Yes. This calculator uses the exact GG33 numerology method taught by Garry Grinberg — the same system used at GG33 Academy. The Life Path calculation follows strict digit-reduction rules and recognizes all three Master Numbers.
What's the difference between Life Path 2 and 11?
Both 2 and 11 reduce to 2, but GG33 treats 11 as a distinct Master Number. In this system, if your path naturally resolves to 2, it is elevated to 11 because of the master-level charisma and intuition it carries. Standard numerology does not always make this distinction — GG33 does.
