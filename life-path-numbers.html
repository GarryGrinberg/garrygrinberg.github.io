How GG33 Calculates Life Path

Full Birth Date Method

GG33 adds every digit of the full birth date together one digit at a time, then reduces the total. It does not reduce month, day, and year separately. If the total becomes 11, 22, or 33, it stays there.

Example from the site: December 25, 1995 becomes 1+2+2+5+1+9+9+5 = 34 → 3+4 = 7.
