Definition

A Life Path number is the foundation of the numerological profile used across GG33. The site explains it as the core number that comes from your full birth date and influences personality, career alignment, relationship compatibility, and financial timing.

In GG33, this is not treated like a novelty result. It is framed as the starting point for understanding your blueprint.

How GG33 Calculates It

Full Birth Date, Digit by Digit

GG33 adds every single digit of the birth date together one at a time. It does not reduce month, day, and year separately. If the final result becomes 11, 22, or 33, those Master Numbers stay as they are.

Example used on the site: December 25, 1995 becomes 1+2+2+5+1+9+9+5 = 34 → 3+4 = 7.
